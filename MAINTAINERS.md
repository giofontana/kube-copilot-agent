## Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for:

- Reviewing and merging pull requests
- Triaging issues and managing the project backlog
- Ensuring CI/CD pipelines are healthy
- Cutting releases and managing versioning
- Guiding the technical direction of the project
- Upholding the [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
